Good Writing is Rewriting!

Hi! I am Abegail B. Gregorio at your service.

    Here are some tips for reading poetry:
  • Take your time and read slowly and deliberately, allowing each word and phrase to sink in fully before moving on.
  • Pay attention to the emotions that the poem evokes in you.
  • Focus on imagery and figurative language used by the poet.
  • Read the poem aloud and listen to your voice, to the sounds the words make.
  • Be attentive to the messages, meanings, allusions, irony, tones of voice, and other nuances in your poem.
