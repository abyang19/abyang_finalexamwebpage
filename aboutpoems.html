    Reading poetry has many benefits, including:

    • Inspiring readers to reflect, dream, reminisce, observe, and fantasize.
    • Encouraging a certain interconnectedness and helping establish a sense of community between oneself and others.
    • Improving vocabulary and verbal dexterity.
    • Providing comfort and boosting mood during periods of stress, trauma and grief.
    • Motivating children to read, building phonemic awareness and building essential skills like vocabulary, fluency, expression, and writing.
